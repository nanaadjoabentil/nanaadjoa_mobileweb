Ashesi University is a liberal arts college - the first of its kind in Ghana - that offers students the opportunity to have a holistic learning experience.

Ashesi was established in 2002, with three rented buildings in Labone serving as the lecture halls and faculty offices

Ashesi moved from Labone to their very own permanent campus in Berekuso in 2010. The campus is a very beautiful one, decorated with nature's best and filled with Africa's smartest.

Currently, Ashesi offers 6 majors: 3 in engineering and three completely different ones.

They are: Mechanical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical/Electronic Engineering, Computer Science, Management Information Systems and Business Administration

Aside from all of the courses required by the various majors, Ashesi offers mandatory courses in math and science, liberal arts, leadership and entrepreneurship to help its students get a head start after graduation
