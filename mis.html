Management Information Systems


Management Information Systems is a combination of Computer Science and Business Administration. Our students who major in M.I.S. get the best of both worlds, as they get to study things in both Computer Science and Business Administration. M.I.S. is intended to shape our students into managers who can do both computer science and business adminstration projects and oversee projects on both sides as well.


Some of the courses M.I.S Majors do are:

Operations Management
Web Technologies
Software Engineering
Financial Accounting
Competitive Strategy
